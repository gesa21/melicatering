Our story

A brother and a sister

I'm Orgesa. My brother Olsi is the cook. He's up at six on the morning of your party, baking focaccia and pressing piadina, and the dolci come from our favourite Italian pasticceria here in Twickenham. I look after everything else, the bookings, the timings, the table. We grew up eating at the same table. Now we set yours.
